Explore VR is a web application that provides state vocational rehabilitation (VR) agencies easy and convenient access to a range of VR and related data for planning, evaluation, and decision-making. Overall, this data-sharing effort seeks to increase knowledge about the public VR program and its role within the larger employment and disability service system within and across states and territories.

Explore VR is part of the Vocational Rehabilitation Research and Training Center (VR-RRTC). The VR-RRTC is a national center that builds the capacity of the public vocational rehabilitation (VR) program to improve employment outcomes for people with disabilities. The VR-RRTC is funded by the National Institute on Disability and Rehabilitation Research and the Rehabilitation Services Administration of the US Department of Education.
